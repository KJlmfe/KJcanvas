<p>Think about the theory of movies. You know, one move is made up of many static pictures. When these pictures quickly switch, it becomes moving. So moving a rectangle learn from that.</p>
<p>The setInterval() method calls a function or evaluates an expression at specified intervals (in milliseconds). Use it to repeat the following steps every 24ms.
<ol>
<li>Clear the canvas.</li>
<li>Set new (x,y) of the rectangle.</li>
<li>Draw the rectangle.</li>
</ol>
