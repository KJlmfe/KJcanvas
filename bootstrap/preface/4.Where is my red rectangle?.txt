	On "3.Move A Rectangle", when you click the "Move it!" button, the red rectangle disappear. Clearly none of us want to see that happen. 
</p>
<p>
It is very easy to solve this problem with a stack. Add the following into codes.
<ul>
<li>Push shapes into the stack after drawing them on canvas.</li>
<li>Draw all shapes in the stack when refresh canvas.</li>
</ul>
